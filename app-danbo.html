So what is Danbo anyway?
Danbo (Japanese: ダンボー) is a Japanese cardboard robot that originally appeared in the manga Yotsuba&! by by Kiyohiko Azuma in 2007.

In Chapter 28, the main character Yotsuba Koiwai mistakes her friend Miura Hayasaka dressed up as a science project for a real robot. When Yotsuba asks the robot its name, Miura does not want to crush her friend’s dreams, naming the costume Danbo, a play on the Japanese word for corrugated cardboard, danboru (段ボール).
